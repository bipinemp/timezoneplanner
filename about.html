About Global Meeting Planner

Making global collaboration effortless, one meeting at a time.

Our Mission

Global Meeting Planner was created to solve one of the most common challenges in our increasingly connected world: finding the perfect time for meetings across different time zones. Whether you're a remote team, globalbusiness, or global freelancer, we make scheduling simple and stress-free.

Why We Built This

As remote work continues to grow, teams are more distributed than ever. We noticed that people were spending too much time manually calculating time differences and struggling to find overlapping work hours. Global Meeting Planner eliminates this friction by providing instant, visual results that make scheduling decisions obvious.
