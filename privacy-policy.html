Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2024

1. Information We Collect

International Meeting Planner is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information to provide our service:

  • Usage Data: We may collect anonymous analytics data about how you use our website to improve our service.
  • Technical Data: Basic technical information like your browser type and device information for compatibility purposes.
  • No Personal Data: We do not collect, store, or process any personal information, email addresses, or account data.

2. How We Use Information

Any data we collect is used solely to:

  • Improve the functionality and user experience of International Meeting Planner
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance our service
  • Ensure technical compatibility across different devices and browsers

3. Data Storage and Security

Your privacy is important to us:

  • All time zone calculations are performed locally in your browser
  • We do not store your meeting preferences or time zone selections
  • No personal data is transmitted to our servers
  • We use industry-standard security measures to protect any data we do collect

4. Third-Party Services

International Meeting Planner may use third-party services for:

  • Analytics: We may use Google Analytics or similar services to understand website usage
  • Advertising: We may display ads through Google AdSense or similar platforms
  • CDN Services: We use content delivery networks to ensure fast loading times

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

5. Cookies and Local Storage

We may use cookies and local storage to:

  • Remember your preferred settings during your session
  • Provide analytics data to improve our service
  • Enable advertising functionality

You can disable cookies in your browser settings, though this may affect some functionality.

6. Children's Privacy

International Meeting Planner does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any material changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

  • Email: privacy@timesyncpro.com
  • Website: Contact Form
